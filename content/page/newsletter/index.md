Emails are sent weekly **only when new posts have been published**. If there are no new posts that week, no email is sent.

## Unsubscribe

Every newsletter email contains a personal unsubscribe link at the bottom — click it and you will be removed immediately with no further steps required.

The unsubscribe link is unique to your subscription, so it cannot be used by anyone else to remove you.
